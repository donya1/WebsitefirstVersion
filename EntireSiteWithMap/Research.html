Research Initiatives

Watch this space for updates on what we are planning.

The COVID-19 Policing and Homelessness Initiative is funded by a grant from the Toronto COVID-19 Action Fund at the University of Toronto. The project is housed in the Sociology department at the University of Toronto Scarborough.

Over the next eight months we will be conducting research across Canada to provide ‘near time’ empirical evidence about how people experiencing homelessness are being policed during the pandemic.
